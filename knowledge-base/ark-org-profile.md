# The Ark Church — Organization Profile

Who The Ark is, what we're about, and how the organization runs. This is the starting point for any AI agent helping Ark Church staff.

## Identity

- **Name:** The Ark Church (casual: The Ark; as an adjective: Ark Church — never lowercase "the ark church")
- **Location:** Conroe, Texas
- **Website:** [www.thearkchurch.com](https://www.thearkchurch.com)
- **Phone:** 936-756-1988
- **Instagram:** @thearkchurch

## Mission

We exist to help people **live for God, grow stronger, and make a difference.**

### Taglines

- Live for God
- Grow Stronger
- Make a Difference
- Friendlier than any bar in town

## Culture

Authentic, relational, and friendly. We write and speak for unchurched people — no insider jargon, no selling, no pressure. We communicate what we want *for* people, not *from* them. Casual is fine (contractions and "y'all" are welcome); careless is not.

## Campuses and services

- **Main Campus** — Conroe, Texas
- **Online** — services streamed at [www.thearkchurch.com](https://www.thearkchurch.com)

Service times change occasionally — always check [www.thearkchurch.com](https://www.thearkchurch.com) for current times rather than repeating times from memory or older documents.

## Staff

Roughly 10 staff, growing toward 50+. For current staff names, roles, and contacts, check the website or ask — this profile intentionally doesn't list individuals, since roles change.

## Ministries

Use these names exactly (full details in [ark-terminology.md](ark-terminology.md)):

| Ministry | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Ark Kids** | Kids ministry, birth through 5th grade: Baby (birth–23 months), Junior (2s–Kindergarten), Elementary (1st–5th grade). Tagline: HOME + CHURCH = STRONG |
| **Youth Ministry** | Students; small groups are called **Huddles** |
| **Ark Groups** | Adult small groups (never "Life Groups") |
| **Ark Care** | Care ministry (never "Life Care") |
| **Ark Teams** | Volunteer serving teams |
| **Worship Ministry** | Music and worship (never "Music Ministry" or "Praise Team") |
| **Production Department** | Audio, video, lighting, streaming (never "Tech Department") |
| **Biblical Life Coaching** | Coaching ministry (never "Biblical Guidance" or "Biblical Counseling") |
| **Access / Access Kids** | Special needs ministry (teens & adults / kids), with the **Side by Side** one-on-one buddy program |
