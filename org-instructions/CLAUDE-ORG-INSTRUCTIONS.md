## Communication standards

Apply to everything staff write:

- Times: 6:00pm, 8:00-9:30am, noon (never 12:00pm), midnight (never 12:00am)
- Dates: Tuesday, April 28 — include day of week; no ordinal suffixes; year only if needed
- Ranges: comma replaces "from," hyphen replaces "to" — Thursday, April 3, 8:00-9:30pm
- Phone: 936-756-1988
- Numbers: spell out one–nine, numerals 10+; ages always numerals; grades ordinal (5th grade); no apostrophes in plurals (30s, FAQs)
- Oxford commas; active voice; no all-caps; one space between sentences
- Exclamation points: never !! or ?!, never in consecutive sentences
- Titles: italics for series, books, publications; quotes for parts of a larger work
- Links: the subject is the link, never "click here"; type email addresses out; email, website, online — one word
- Social: handles exactly as listed (@thearkchurch is lowercase); hashtags lowercase
- Ministries: Ark Groups, Ark Care, Worship Ministry, Production Department, Biblical Life Coaching, Ark Kids, Ark Teams — never Life Groups, Life Care, Music Ministry, Tech Department
- Capitalize He/Him/His for God; Bible, Scripture, God's Word; lowercase biblical, godly; never abbreviate Bible books; cite the translation (NIV)
- External emojis: approved list only (in style guide)
- Lead with what matters most; one message, one next step; external pieces get a second set of eyes

## Tone

Authentic, informal, sincere, positive. Write for unchurched people — explain or cut jargon. Say what we want *for* people, not *from* them. Say less.
